Each archetype represents a unique **relational signature** - a distinct way of experiencing and nurturing connection. Reflection prompts should honor these signatures, inviting users to capture what makes each friendship irreplaceable.

## Reflection as Ritual

The archetype system transforms reflection from generic note-taking into a **ritual of honoring relational diversity**. Each friend requires different questions because each friendship has a different **soul**.

When Emma reflects on coffee with Sarah (High Priestess), she's asked about **truth and depth**.
When she reflects on a party with Marcus (Sun), she's asked about **joy and radiance**.
When she reflects on a project with Kai (Magician), she's asked about **creation and possibility**.

This teaches users to **see their friends more clearly** - to appreciate that different people feed different parts of our soul, and all of it matters.

That's the magic of the archetype-aware reflection system.
